My Active Settings — Zone Detection

JASON'S CONFIG
Swing Length30Higher than default (12) → fewer but much stronger zones. Only major institutional pivots qualify.
Max Active Zones15Default is 8. With 15 zones the full picture from Daily bias down to M5 is visible at once.
Max Zone Height (ATR)0.5Very tight ceiling vs default 3.0. Only razor-narrow, precise zones are accepted.
